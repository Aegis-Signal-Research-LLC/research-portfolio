# AI-Driven Voice Cloning and Deepfake Threats in VoIP Systems

## Overview
This research explores the use of artificial intelligence in generating synthetic voice signals and its implications for Voice over IP (VoIP) communication systems.

## Problem
Modern AI models can replicate human voice patterns with high accuracy, creating risks for authentication systems and communication integrity.

## Key Risks
- Voice impersonation attacks
- Social engineering using synthetic speech
- Bypass of voice-based authentication systems

## Research Focus
- Detection of AI-generated voice signals
- Security weaknesses in VoIP systems
- Threat modeling for voice-based attacks

## Future Work
- Development of detection mechanisms
- Integration of AI-based defense systems
