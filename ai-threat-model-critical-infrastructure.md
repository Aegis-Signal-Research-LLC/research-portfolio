# AI-Driven Threat Model for Critical Infrastructure Networks

## Overview
This research focuses on the development of threat models that incorporate artificial intelligence in analyzing and predicting cyber attacks on critical infrastructure systems.

## Problem
Critical infrastructure systems such as energy, telecommunications, and defense networks are increasingly targeted by advanced cyber threats. The integration of AI in attack strategies introduces new levels of complexity and unpredictability.

## Key Risks
- AI-assisted attack automation
- Increased attack precision and scalability
- Exploitation of infrastructure vulnerabilities

## Research Focus
- Threat modeling for AI-driven cyber attacks
- Risk assessment for critical infrastructure systems
- Identification of high-impact vulnerabilities

## Future Work
- Development of predictive defense models
- Integration of AI-based monitoring systems
